# 03 · Aesthetic Research — 审美研究

> 收集是起点，不是终点。真正的工作是从数据中读出规律，从规律中提炼原则。
> Collecting is only the beginning. The real work is reading patterns from data, then distilling them into principles.

---

## 为什么不是 Moodboard / Why Not a Moodboard

Moodboard 收集感觉，Aesthetic Research 研究感觉。

前者问"这张图好看吗"，后者问"我为什么总是被这类图吸引"。
前者产出一块情绪拼贴，后者产出可以迁移、可以决策的审美原则。

*A moodboard collects feelings. Aesthetic research studies them.*

*One asks "does this look good?" The other asks "why am I always drawn to this type of thing?"*
*One produces a collage. The other produces principles that can travel into every future decision.*

---

## 研究方法 / Methodology

```
Step 1 · 收集 Collect
  在每个维度里，收集 10–20 个你喜欢的样本。
  不过滤，不解释，先收集。
  Gather 10–20 samples per dimension. No filtering, no explaining. Just collect.

      ↓

Step 2 · 标注 Tag
  用统一的标签词汇，给每个样本打 2–3 个标签。
  目的是把"感觉"变成可以统计的数据。
  Tag each sample with 2–3 words from a shared vocabulary.
  The goal is to make feelings countable.

      ↓

Step 3 · 找模式 Find Patterns
  统计哪些标签出现频率最高。
  挑出 5 个最能代表你审美的样本，深入描述。
  Count which tags appear most often.
  Pick 5 samples that best represent your taste and describe them in depth.

      ↓

Step 4 · 提炼原则 Distill Principles
  把频率最高的模式，转化成可以指导决策的原则句子。
  输入到 08-patterns.md，再从那里流向 04-Visual-Language.md。
  Turn the most frequent patterns into principle statements that guide decisions.
  Feed them into 08-patterns.md, which flows into 04-Visual-Language.md.
```

## 研究顺序建议 / Suggested Order

不必按编号顺序。从你材料最多、最熟悉的维度开始。
`08-patterns.md` 最后写，但可以随时往里记笔记。

*You don't have to follow the numbered order. Start with whatever dimension you have the most material for.*
*Write 08-patterns.md last, but feel free to jot notes into it at any time.*
