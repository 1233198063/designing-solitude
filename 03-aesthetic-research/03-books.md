# 03 · Books — 书籍

> 一本书在你手里的重量，是它最先说的话。
> The weight of a book in your hand is the first thing it says to you.

---

## 标签词汇表 / Tag Vocabulary

这里的"书"包括两个层面：作为物件的书（封面、版式、装帧）和作为阅读体验的书（文字密度、留白、节奏）。
*"Books" here covers two layers: the book as object (cover, layout, binding) and as reading experience (text density, white space, pacing).*

```
封面 / Cover
  photographic · illustrated · typographic · abstract · minimal · collage · handmade · plain

色彩 / Color
  dark · light · monochrome · colorful · muted · single-color · earth · black-white

版式 / Interior layout
  airy · dense · generous-margin · illustrated · footnote-rich · plain · poetic-spacing · traditional

装帧 / Format
  hardcover · paperback · pocket · oversized · sewn · special-edition · softcover-flap

类型感 / Genre feel
  literary · essay · poetry · academic · memoir · how-to · philosophy · nature-writing

情绪 / Mood
  contemplative · lyrical · intimate · provocative · grounding · expansive · quiet · urgent
```
