# 02 · Core Values — 核心价值观 / Core Values

> 价值观是品牌所有决策的隐形标准。当我不确定该不该发一篇内容、该不该用一种颜色时，回来问这里。
> Values are the invisible standard behind every brand decision. When unsure whether to publish a piece or use a color, come here first.

---

## 价值观定义 / Value Definitions

用下面的格式定义 3–5 个核心词。每个词都需要正面定义和反面定义，因为一个词可以指向很多方向。

*Define 3–5 core words using the format below. Each needs both a positive and a negative definition, because a single word can point in many directions.*

---

### 01 · 善良 / Kindness

#### 我指的是 / I mean

- 善良是一种主动的选择，而不是被迫的行为。
- 在尊重自己和他人的前提下，给予理解、善意和帮助。
- 有边界地善良，而不是无限地付出。

- Kindness is a conscious choice, not something done out of pressure or obligation.
- Treating people with empathy, respect, and generosity while also respecting my own boundaries.
- Choosing kindness without losing myself.

#### 我不是指 / I don't mean

- 因为害怕冲突而一味退让。
- 没有原则地迎合别人。
- 为了维持表面的和谐而委屈自己。

- Giving in because I'm afraid of conflict.
- Pleasing everyone at the expense of myself.
- Sacrificing my own boundaries just to keep the peace.


---

### 02 · Curiosity / 好奇

#### 我指的是 / I mean

- 对人与世界保持好奇。
- 我的很多文章都始于一个简单的问题："为什么？"
- 在下结论之前先观察，在评价之前先理解。
- 希望一直保持学习和探索的能力。

- Staying curious about people, nature, art, and everyday life.
- Most of my writing begins with a simple question: *Why?*
- Observing before judging, and understanding before concluding.
- Remaining open to learning throughout life.

#### 我不是指 / I don't mean

- 为了新鲜感而不断追逐热点。
- 只收集信息，却没有自己的思考。
- 被碎片化的信息不断分散注意力。

- Chasing novelty for its own sake.
- Collecting information without reflection.
- Being constantly distracted by endless new information.



---

### 03 · Courage / 勇气

#### 我指的是 / I mean

- 即使不确定，也愿意继续创作。
- 真诚表达自己的想法和感受。
- 愿意尝试新的生活方式，也愿意承认自己的改变。

- Continuing to create even when I'm uncertain.
- Expressing my own thoughts honestly.
- Being willing to try new things and embrace change.

#### 我不是指 / I don't mean

- 鲁莽。
- 逞强。
- 假装自己什么都懂。

- Being reckless.
- Pretending to be fearless.
- Acting as if I already have all the answers.


---

### 04 · Presence / 活在当下

#### 我指的是 / I mean

- 真正关注眼前的生活。
- 从散步、书法、阅读、音乐、植物等日常体验中发现美。
- 希望我的作品能够让人慢下来，重新感受生活。

- Paying attention to the present moment.
- Discovering beauty in ordinary experiences like walking, calligraphy, reading, music, and nature.
- Creating work that encourages people to slow down and reconnect with life.

#### 我不是指 / I don't mean

- 逃避现实。
- 只追求短暂的放松。
- 为了吸引注意力而创作没有思考空间的娱乐内容。

- Escaping reality.
- Seeking comfort without reflection.
- Creating shallow entertainment simply to capture attention.


### 05 · Helping Others / 助人

#### 我指的是 / I mean

- 分享自己的经历，希望给别人一些力量或新的视角。
- 如果我的文章能让一个人觉得："原来我并不孤单。"
  我就觉得很值得。

- Sharing my experiences in the hope that someone finds comfort, encouragement, or a new perspective.
- If even one person finishes reading and feels,
  *"Maybe I'm not alone after all,"*
  then my writing has already achieved something meaningful.

#### 我不是指 / I don't mean

- 试图改变别人。
- 未经请求就给别人建议。
- 认为自己的经历适用于所有人。

- Trying to fix other people's lives.
- Giving unsolicited advice.
- Assuming my experiences are universal.

## 价值观冲突测试 / Values Conflict Test

> 当两个价值观产生矛盾时，哪个优先？提前做好决定。
> When two values conflict, which takes priority? Decide in advance.

### 当"真实"和"精致"冲突时
### When authenticity and polish conflict

☑ 我选择 **真实（Authenticity）**。

因为真实比完美更重要。

☑ I choose **authenticity**.

Real expression matters more than perfection.

---

### 当"分享"和"隐私"冲突时
### When sharing and privacy conflict

☑ 我选择 **隐私（Privacy）**。

真实并不意味着公开生活中的每一个细节。

☑ I choose **privacy**.

Authenticity does not require exposing every detail of my life.

---

### 当"深度"和"传播性"冲突时
### When depth and shareability conflict

☑ 我选择 **深度（Depth）**。

我更希望真正打动少数人，而不是短暂吸引很多人。

☑ I choose **depth**.

I'd rather deeply resonate with a few people than briefly impress many.

---

### 当"美感"和"诚实"冲突时
### When beauty and honesty conflict

☑ 我选择 **诚实（Honesty）**。

美感应该服务于表达，而不是掩盖真实。

☑ I choose **honesty**.

Beauty should support truth, never replace it.

---

### 当"一致性"和"成长"冲突时
### When consistency and growth conflict

☑ 我选择 **成长（Growth）**。

品牌应该随着我的成长不断演化，而不是把我困在过去。

☑ I choose **growth**.

A living brand should evolve as I evolve.
